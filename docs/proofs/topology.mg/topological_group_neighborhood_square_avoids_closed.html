Let G, Tg, mult, inv, e and F be given.
Assume HTg: topology_on G Tg.
Assume Hmultfun: function_on mult (setprod G G) G.
Assume Hinvfun: function_on inv G G.
Assume HeG: e ∈ G.
Assume Hassoc: ∀x y z : set, x ∈ G → y ∈ G → z ∈ G → apply_fun mult (apply_fun mult (x,y),z) = apply_fun mult (x,apply_fun mult (y,z)).
Assume Hid: ∀x : set, x ∈ G → apply_fun mult (e,x) = x ∧ apply_fun mult (x,e) = x.
Assume HinvAx: ∀x : set, x ∈ G → apply_fun mult (x,apply_fun inv x) = e ∧ apply_fun mult (apply_fun inv x,x) = e.
Assume Hmultcont: continuous_map (setprod G G) (product_topology G Tg G Tg) G Tg mult.
Assume Hinvcont: continuous_map G Tg G Tg inv.
Assume HFcl: closed_in G Tg F.
Assume HenF: e ∉ F.
Set U to be the term G ∖ F.
We prove the intermediate claim HUopen: open_in G Tg U.
An exact proof term for the current goal is (open_of_closed_complement G Tg F HFcl).
We prove the intermediate claim HUinTg: U ∈ Tg.
An exact proof term for the current goal is (andER (topology_on G Tg) (U ∈ Tg) HUopen).
We prove the intermediate claim HeU: e ∈ U.
Apply (setminusI G F e HeG) to the current goal.
Assume HeF: e ∈ F.
An exact proof term for the current goal is (HenF HeF).
We prove the intermediate claim HexV: ∃V : set, V ∈ Tg ∧ e ∈ V ∧ (∀x : set, x ∈ V → apply_fun inv x ∈ V) ∧ ∀x y : set, x ∈ V → y ∈ V → apply_fun mult (x,y) ∈ U.
An exact proof term for the current goal is (topological_group_symmetric_neighborhood_square G Tg mult inv e U HTg Hmultfun Hinvfun HeG Hassoc Hid HinvAx Hmultcont Hinvcont HUinTg HeU).
Apply HexV to the current goal.
Let V be given.
Assume HVpack.
We use V to witness the existential quantifier.
Apply andI to the current goal.
We prove the intermediate claim HABC: (V ∈ Tg ∧ e ∈ V) ∧ (∀x : set, x ∈ V → apply_fun inv x ∈ V).
An exact proof term for the current goal is (andEL ((V ∈ Tg ∧ e ∈ V) ∧ (∀x : set, x ∈ V → apply_fun inv x ∈ V)) (∀x y : set, x ∈ V → y ∈ V → apply_fun mult (x,y) ∈ U) HVpack).
An exact proof term for the current goal is HABC.
Let x be given.
Let y be given.
Assume HxV: x ∈ V.
Assume HyV: y ∈ V.
We will prove apply_fun mult (x,y) ∉ F.
We prove the intermediate claim HmultMap: ∀x0 y0 : set, x0 ∈ V → y0 ∈ V → apply_fun mult (x0,y0) ∈ U.
An exact proof term for the current goal is (andER ((V ∈ Tg ∧ e ∈ V) ∧ (∀x0 : set, x0 ∈ V → apply_fun inv x0 ∈ V)) (∀x0 y0 : set, x0 ∈ V → y0 ∈ V → apply_fun mult (x0,y0) ∈ U) HVpack).
We prove the intermediate claim HmultU: apply_fun mult (x,y) ∈ U.
An exact proof term for the current goal is (HmultMap x y HxV HyV).
Assume HmultF: apply_fun mult (x,y) ∈ F.
We prove the intermediate claim HmultNotU: ¬ (apply_fun mult (x,y) ∈ U).
Assume HmultUin: apply_fun mult (x,y) ∈ U.
We prove the intermediate claim HnotF: apply_fun mult (x,y) ∉ F.
An exact proof term for the current goal is (setminusE2 G F (apply_fun mult (x,y)) HmultUin).
An exact proof term for the current goal is (HnotF HmultF).
An exact proof term for the current goal is (HmultNotU HmultU).
∎
