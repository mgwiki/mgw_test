(*** helper: right translation maps open sets to open sets ***)
(*** LATEX VERSION: If R_a(x)=x·a is a homeomorphism of a topological group, then it carries open sets to open sets. ***)
L266235
Let G, Tg, mult, inv, e, a and U be given.
L266236
Assume HTg: topology_on G Tg.
L266237
Assume Hmultcont: continuous_map (setprod G G) (product_topology G Tg G Tg) G Tg mult.
L266238
Assume Hinvfun: function_on inv G G.
L266239
Assume HeG: e ∈ G.
L266240
Assume Hassoc: ∀x y z : set, x ∈ G → y ∈ G → z ∈ G → apply_fun mult (apply_fun mult (x,y),z) = apply_fun mult (x,apply_fun mult (y,z)).
L266244
Assume Hid: ∀x : set, x ∈ G → apply_fun mult (e,x) = x ∧ apply_fun mult (x,e) = x.
L266245
Assume Hinv: ∀x : set, x ∈ G → apply_fun mult (x,apply_fun inv x) = e ∧ apply_fun mult (apply_fun inv x,x) = e.
L266248
Assume HaG: a ∈ G.
L266249
Assume HU: U ∈ Tg.
L266250
Set Ra to be the term (compose_fun G (pair_map G {(x,x)|x ∈ G} (const_fun G a)) mult).
L266251
We prove the intermediate claim Hhom: homeomorphism G Tg G Tg Ra.
L266253
An exact proof term for the current goal is (right_translation_homeomorphism G Tg mult inv e a HTg Hmultcont Hinvfun HeG Hassoc Hid Hinv HaG).
L266254
An exact proof term for the current goal is (homeomorphism_image_open G Tg G Tg Ra U Hhom HU).
∎
