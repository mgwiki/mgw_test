(*** helper: setmul_by is open in a topological group ***)
(*** LATEX VERSION: If U and V are open in a topological group, then U·V is open; in our encoding, setmul_by mult U V is open. ***)
L269008
Let G, Tg, mult, inv, e, U and V be given.
L269009
Assume HTg: topology_on G Tg.
L269010
Assume Hmultcont: continuous_map (setprod G G) (product_topology G Tg G Tg) G Tg mult.
L269011
Assume Hinvfun: function_on inv G G.
L269012
Assume HeG: e ∈ G.
L269013
Assume Hassoc: ∀x y z : set, x ∈ G → y ∈ G → z ∈ G → apply_fun mult (apply_fun mult (x,y),z) = apply_fun mult (x,apply_fun mult (y,z)).
L269017
Assume Hid: ∀x : set, x ∈ G → apply_fun mult (e,x) = x ∧ apply_fun mult (x,e) = x.
L269018
Assume HinvAx: ∀x : set, x ∈ G → apply_fun mult (x,apply_fun inv x) = e ∧ apply_fun mult (apply_fun inv x,x) = e.
L269020
Assume HU: U ∈ Tg.
L269021
Assume HV: V ∈ Tg.
L269022
rewrite the current goal using setmul_by_def (from left to right).
L269023
An exact proof term for the current goal is (topological_group_mult_image_open G Tg mult inv e U V HTg Hmultcont Hinvfun HeG Hassoc Hid HinvAx HU HV).
∎
