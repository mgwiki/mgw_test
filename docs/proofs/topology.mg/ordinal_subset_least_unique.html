(*** helper: least element of a subset is unique ***)
(*** LATEX VERSION: The least element of a subset (if it exists) is unique. ***)
L197170
Let I, S, m1 and m2 be given.
L197171
Assume _HordI: ordinal I.
L197172
Assume _HSsubI: S ⊆ I.
L197173
Assume Hm1S: m1 ∈ S.
L197174
Assume Hm1least: ∀s : set, s ∈ S → m1 ⊆ s.
L197175
Assume Hm2S: m2 ∈ S.
L197176
Assume Hm2least: ∀s : set, s ∈ S → m2 ⊆ s.
L197177
Apply set_ext to the current goal.
L197179
An exact proof term for the current goal is (Hm1least m2 Hm2S).
L197180
An exact proof term for the current goal is (Hm2least m1 Hm1S).
∎
