Let G, Tg, mult, inv, e, a and U be given.
Assume HTg: topology_on G Tg.
Assume Hmultcont: continuous_map (setprod G G) (product_topology G Tg G Tg) G Tg mult.
Assume Hinvfun: function_on inv G G.
Assume HeG: e ∈ G.
Assume Hassoc: ∀x y z : set, x ∈ G → y ∈ G → z ∈ G → apply_fun mult (apply_fun mult (x,y),z) = apply_fun mult (x,apply_fun mult (y,z)).
Assume Hid: ∀x : set, x ∈ G → apply_fun mult (e,x) = x ∧ apply_fun mult (x,e) = x.
Assume Hinv: ∀x : set, x ∈ G → apply_fun mult (x,apply_fun inv x) = e ∧ apply_fun mult (apply_fun inv x,x) = e.
Assume HaG: a ∈ G.
Assume HU: U ∈ Tg.
Set La to be the term (compose_fun G (pair_map G (const_fun G a) {(x,x)|x ∈ G}) mult).
We prove the intermediate claim Hhom: homeomorphism G Tg G Tg La.
An exact proof term for the current goal is (left_translation_homeomorphism G Tg mult inv e a HTg Hmultcont Hinvfun HeG Hassoc Hid Hinv HaG).
An exact proof term for the current goal is (homeomorphism_image_open G Tg G Tg La U Hhom HU).
∎
