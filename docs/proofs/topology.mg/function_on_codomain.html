(*** helper: enlarge the codomain of a function_on ***)
(*** LATEX VERSION: Codomain enlargement: if f:X->Y and Y subset Z then f:X->Z. ***)
L67690
Let f, X, Y and Z be given.
L67691

L67692
Assume HYZ: Y ⊆ Z.
L67693

L67694

L67694
